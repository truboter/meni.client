## 1. What are cookies?
Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device (computer, smartphone, tablet) when you visit a website or use an application. Cookies are widely used to make websites and applications work more efficiently and provide information to the owners.
### Types of cookies by lifespan:
- **Session cookies** – temporary cookies that expire when you close your browser or app
- **Persistent cookies** – cookies that remain on your device for a set period or until you delete them
### Types of cookies by purpose:
- **Strictly necessary cookies** – essential for the website/app to function
- **Performance cookies** – collect information about how you use the website/app
- **Functional cookies** – remember your choices and preferences
- **Targeting/advertising cookies** – used to deliver relevant advertising (we do not use these)
## 2. Similar technologies
In addition to cookies, we may use similar technologies such as:
- **Local storage** – data stored in your browser or app for functionality and preferences
- **Session storage** – temporary data stored during your browsing session
- **Device identifiers** – unique identifiers assigned to your device or app installation
- **SDKs (Software Development Kits)** – code libraries that may collect usage and technical data
- **Pixels and beacons** – small images or code snippets used to track events
Throughout this Policy, we refer to cookies and similar technologies collectively as "cookies" unless otherwise specified.

## 6. How to manage cookies
You have several options to control and manage cookies:
### 6.1 Cookie consent banner
When you first visit the Customer Application, you will see a cookie consent banner allowing you to:
- Accept all cookies
- Accept only essential cookies
- Customize your cookie preferences
You can change your preferences at any time through the cookie settings available in the app/website footer.
### 6.2 Browser settings
Most browsers allow you to:
- View and delete cookies
- Block all cookies
- Block third-party cookies
- Receive warnings before cookies are set
**How to manage cookies in popular browsers:**
- **Google Chrome:** Settings > Privacy and security > Cookies and other site data
- **Mozilla Firefox:** Settings > Privacy & Security > Cookies and Site Data
- **Safari:** Preferences > Privacy > Manage Website Data
- **Microsoft Edge:** Settings > Cookies and site permissions > Manage and delete cookies
- **Brave:** Settings > Shields > Cookies and other site data
For more information, visit: https://www.aboutcookies.org or https://www.allaboutcookies.org
### 6.3 Mobile app settings
For mobile apps, you can manage:
- **iOS:** Settings > [App Name] > Clear Data / Reset
- **Android:** Settings > Apps > [App Name] > Storage > Clear Data
### 6.4 Opt-out of analytics
You can opt out of specific analytics services:
- **Google Analytics:** https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout
- **Meta Pixel:** https://www.facebook.com/settings?tab=ads
- **Device advertising ID:**
- iOS: Settings > Privacy > Advertising > Limit Ad Tracking
- Android: Settings > Google > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalization
### 6.5 Do Not Track (DNT)
Some browsers support "Do Not Track" signals. Our Service does not currently respond to DNT signals, but you can manage tracking through cookie consent and browser settings as described above.
